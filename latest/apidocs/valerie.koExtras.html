<static> isolatedBindingHandler(initOrUpdateFunction, updateFunction) → {Object}

Creates a binding handler where the update method is only invoked if one of its observable or computed dependencies is updated. Unlike normal bindings, the update method is not invoked if a sibling binding is updated.
